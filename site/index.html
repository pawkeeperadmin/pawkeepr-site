FAQ

Warm, practical answers.

How private is my data?

Private by default. Your data is stored securely and is only shared when you explicitly initiate an action. See the Privacy Policy.

How does AI summarisation work?

AI features are optional. When enabled, they can help extract and summarise documents you choose to share. You review suggestions before saving.

Can my family help log care?

Yes. You can invite household members and assign roles. View-only roles can browse without making changes.

Will I receive notifications offline?

Notifications depend on device settings and connectivity. If you’re offline, the app may delay syncing until you’re back online.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your data from within the app, and you can request account deletion at any time.

What happens if there’s no connectivity?

We’ll surface a clear “no connectivity” state and offer a retry. An offline-first mode is planned.
