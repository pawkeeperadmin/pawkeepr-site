FAQ

Warm, practical answers.

How private is my data?

Private by default. Your data is stored securely and only shared when you explicitly initiate an action. See Privacy Policy.

How does AI work?

AI features are optional. When enabled, they can help extract and summarize documents you share with the app. You review suggestions before saving.

Can my family help log care?

Yes. You can invite household members and assign roles. View-only roles can browse without making changes.

How much does it cost?

The beta is free. If pricing changes later, we'll keep it simple and clearly communicated.

Which pets are supported?

Dogs, cats, and more. The app is built around flexible care records, not a single species template.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your data from within the app, and you can request account deletion at any time.
