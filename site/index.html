A thoughtful home for your pet's story.

pawkeepr keeps your pet's care history, reminders, documents, and memories in one calm place. Built for clarity, continuity, and peace of mind.

Timeline that feels like a story

Log vet visits, medications, symptoms, grooming, milestones, and everyday notes. Keep everything searchable and connected.

Reminders that keep you on track

Schedule upcoming care and get notifications when it's time. Mark items done and stay organised.

Documents and attachments

Attach invoices, PDFs, and photos so important details are never buried in email threads again.

Family sharing

Invite household members so the people who care can log care together, with role-based access.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Is pawkeepr veterinary advice?

No. pawkeepr helps you organise records and reminders, but it is not a substitute for professional veterinary care.

What data do you collect?

We collect the data needed to run the app, such as your account email and the pet records, photos, and documents you choose to upload. See Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion in the app under Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.

How do I report a security issue?

Please email security@pawkeepr.cc. Include steps to reproduce and any supporting details.
