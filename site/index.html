FAQ

Get Early Access
Is Pawkeepr free during beta?

Yes. The iOS TestFlight beta is currently free.

Do I need an account?

Yes. You need an account so your pets, reminders, and documents stay securely tied to you.

Can I track multiple pets?

Yes. Pawkeepr supports multiple pets with separate histories and reminders.

Can I turn AI off completely?

Yes. AI features are optional and can be disabled from settings.

How private is my data?

Private by default. Your data is stored securely and is only shared when you explicitly initiate an action.

How does AI summarisation work?

When enabled, AI can help extract and summarise documents you choose to process. You review suggestions before saving.

Can my family help log care?

Yes. You can invite household members and assign roles. View-only roles can browse without making changes.
