Terms of Use

Medical disclaimer

Pawkeepr is not veterinary advice and is not a substitute for professional veterinary care. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for diagnosis, treatment, and emergencies.

Accounts

You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials and for activity under your account.

Acceptable use

Please do not misuse the service, attempt to access systems without authorisation, or upload malicious content.

Account deletion

You can request account deletion within the app (Settings → Privacy → Delete Account). If a grace period is available, the app will show it clearly.

Privacy Policy

Clear, practical, and written for real use.

What we collect

Account: email address, display name, authentication provider identity, preferences, notification settings.

Pet and care records: pet profiles, timeline entries, reminders, caregiver details, notes.

Attachments: photos and documents you choose to upload.

Identifiers: internal user identifiers and device tokens for notifications (if enabled).

Diagnostics: crash and reliability signals to keep the app stable.

How we use it

To run the app, sync your data, send the notifications you enable, provide support, troubleshoot issues, and protect service integrity.

AI features

AI features are optional. When enabled, they can process documents you choose to share with the app to help create summaries. You can disable AI at any time in-app.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with service providers as needed to run the service, when required by law, or when you explicitly initiate an action.

Your choices

You can export your data and request account deletion from within the app (Settings → Privacy).

Contact

Support: support@pawkeepr.cc
