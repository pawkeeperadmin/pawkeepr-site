Support

If you need help, email support@pawkeepr.cc. Include your device type (iPhone/Android) and what you were doing when the issue occurred.

FAQ

I am not receiving notifications

Check Settings → Notifications in the app, and iOS Settings → Notifications → pawkeepr. Also confirm Focus/Do Not Disturb isn't blocking alerts.

How do I share access with family?

Open Settings → Family Sharing → Manage Family Group and invite a member. Roles control whether members can edit or view.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.
