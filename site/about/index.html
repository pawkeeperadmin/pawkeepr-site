About pawkeepr

Version 1.3.0 (11)

I built Pawkeepr because I realised how much of Rumi’s life, the little milestones, the vet visits, the worries, the wins, was scattered across photos, notes, emails and memory. I wanted one calm, trusted place where everything about her life could live together, not just as records, but as a story.

This app was created out of care: care for the moments that matter, for the details that help keep our pets healthy, and for the peace of mind that comes from knowing nothing important is forgotten. If it helps even one family feel more organised, more confident, and more connected to their pet’s journey, then it has done exactly what it was built to do.
