Built from care.

I built pawkeepr after realising how much of Rumi's life, the little milestones, the vet visits, the worries, the wins, was scattered across photos, notes, emails and memory. I wanted one calm, trusted place where everything could live together, not just as records, but as a story, and where nothing important is forgotten.
