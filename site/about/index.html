About pawkeepr

Version 1.3.0 (11)

Built from care, for calm and clarity in everyday pet care.

Our Mission

Pet care should feel steady, not scattered.

pawkeepr exists to give pet owners a calm, organised way to manage their pet’s life — from vet visits to daily care.

What Makes pawkeepr Different

Most tools either:

  • Focus only on reminders
  • Store notes without structure
  • Over-automate with AI
  • Or feel clinical and complicated

pawkeepr is different.

It’s timeline-first, privacy-first, and built for real day-to-day use.

Built for Trust

We designed pawkeepr around predictable behaviour:

  • Nothing auto-saves without review
  • AI is optional
  • Your data can be exported
  • Your account can be deleted anytime

We believe clarity builds trust.

Our Approach

We’re building pawkeepr thoughtfully — not rapidly.

We care more about reliability and clarity than adding flashy features.

If something feels confusing, we treat that as a design problem to solve.

Where We Are Today

pawkeepr is currently available via iOS TestFlight public beta.

We’re improving it continuously based on user feedback.

If pawkeepr sounds useful for your household, we’d love for you to try it.
