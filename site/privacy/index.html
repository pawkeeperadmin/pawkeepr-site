Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what pawkeepr collects, how it is used, and what controls you have.

Last updated

February 2026

Information we collect

Account information: email address, display name, authentication provider identity, preferences, notification settings.

Pet and care information: pet profiles, timeline entries, reminders, caregiver details, notes.

User content: photos and documents you upload (attachments such as PDFs/invoices).

Identifiers: internal user IDs and device tokens used to deliver notifications.

Diagnostics: basic crash and performance diagnostics to keep the app reliable.

How we use your information

To run core app features and sync your data, send the notifications you enable, provide support, troubleshoot issues, and protect service integrity.

Sharing and service providers

We do not sell personal data. We use service providers for infrastructure, authentication, notifications, and (where enabled by you) optional AI/processing features.

Your choices

You can update your settings in-app. You can request account deletion within the app (Settings → Privacy → Delete Account).
